SafePrompt — Privacy Policy

Privacy at a glance: Short, clear, and local-first.

What we store
  • Local token mappings in the browser session vault (temporary).
  • No prompts/responses are sent to our servers by default.
What we do not collect
  • No prompt or response content is transmitted to SafePrompt servers.
  • No token-to-real mapping is uploaded.
Support diagnostics
  • Optional, user-initiated diagnostics only (counts and metadata).
