Frequently asked questions
Quick answers for v1.
How does Secure Send work?
  • We replace detected PII with stable tokens (e.g., [[SP_EMAIL_1]]) before sending.
How does Reveal Original Data work?
  • We restore tokens locally in your browser using the session vault.
What happens on refresh?
  • Mappings persist for a limited time per tab (session vault TTL).
